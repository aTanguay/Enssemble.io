Enssemble

Wireless synthesizer units placed around a room. Each one a different instrument. Your iPhone is the bandleader. The room is the mix.

Your room becomes the band.

Forget stereo. Forget headphones. Enssemble puts real synthesizers in physical space — one on the bookshelf, one by the window, one in the corner.

Each unit connects wirelessly via Bluetooth MIDI. Your iPhone drives them all. Sound comes from where the instruments are, not where the speakers are.

It's spatial audio without the algorithms. Just physics.

How It Works

Stupidly simple.

No Wi-Fi. No DAW. No computer. Just power, Bluetooth, and an iPhone.

1
⚗
Flash a board
Upload firmware to an AMYboard via USB. Pick a band member config — that sets the name, channel, and default sound.
2
⚙
Place it somewhere
On a shelf. By the window. In the corner. Plug in power and a speaker. The unit boots and starts advertising over Bluetooth.
3
☎
Connect from iOS
Open AUM on your iPhone or iPad. Each unit appears as a BLE MIDI device. Tap to connect. iOS handles 13+ connections.
4
♫
Play the room
Route MIDI channels to each member. Use a keyboard app, a sequencer, or our Mozaic control surface. The room is your mix.
